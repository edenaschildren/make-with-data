<h2>Make-With-Data: Mapping Mental Health Repository</h2>
<!-- Insert links to our websites here-->

<p>Make-With-Data supports the research of community-based challenges, primarily related to local conservation, sustainability, and mental health.</p>


<p>The workshops are full of activities that align real world challenges with data sources and scientific methods to explore solutions that simultaneously teach important mapping and data science skills.</p>


<p>We explore patterns in mental health data and address questions such as where are people experiencing depression? What does access to mental health supprt look like? Are mental health conditions (anxiety, depression, ADHD, schizophrenia, etc.) related to socioeconomic and environmental variables? </p>
